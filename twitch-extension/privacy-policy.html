SACK BATTLE - Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 21, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SACK BATTLE ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Twitch Extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Twitch User Information

  • Twitch User ID: Used to identify you and link your Twitch account to your game character
  • Twitch Username: Used for display purposes in the game
  • Channel ID: Used to associate your actions with the appropriate game session

2.2 Game Activity Data

  • Game Commands: Actions you take in the game (attack, heal, cover, etc.)
  • Game Progress: Your burst gauge level and other game statistics
  • Timestamps: When you perform actions to prevent spam and manage cooldowns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide the game functionality and connect your Twitch account to your game character
  • Track your game progress and statistics
  • Prevent spam and abuse through rate limiting
  • Display your username and actions to other players in the game
  • Improve the game experience and fix technical issues

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Game Display: Your username and game actions are visible to other players in the same game session
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored temporarily in memory for the duration of your game session
  • User mappings may be cached temporarily to improve performance
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
  • No payment information or sensitive personal data is collected or stored

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the game service:

  • Game session data is cleared when the game ends
  • User mappings may be retained for up to 30 days to improve user experience
  • Error logs containing user IDs are automatically purged after 7 days

7. Third-Party Services

Our extension interacts with:

  • Twitch Platform: We receive user tokens and IDs from Twitch's authorization system
  • Hosting Services: Our backend may be hosted on cloud platforms (Railway, Vercel, etc.)

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the extension at any time
  • Request information about what data we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data (contact us below)

9. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
